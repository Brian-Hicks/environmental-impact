Online Help:

The Environmental Impact Assessment application allows the public and individuals within an organization to determine potential impacts of development on the natural environment.

Finding Information

Individuals and organizations can use the Environmental Impact Assessment application to define an area of interest based on a particular development project, and generate a report of what environmental features may be impacted by that project. Results can be viewed directly within the application, or can be downloaded for further distribution and use.

Map Based Navigation

There are several ways to interact with the map:

  1. Tap a point of interest you'd like to see
  2. Hold down the shift key and drag a box on the map to zoom in
  3. Hold down Ctrl+Shift to zoom out
  4. Roll your mouse roller to zoom in/out

Searching

Define your area of interest (AOI) to discover environmental features:

  • Use the Placename option to define your area of interest using an address
  • Use drawing tools or select features to define your area of interest
  • Define your area of interest by uploading a zipped shapefile
  • Use coordinates and a series of bearings and distances to define your area of interest

Click on the Report panel to view a summary of environmental features that may be affected by development in your defined AOI. View results directly in the application, or download summary reports for further distribution and use.

Browser Compatibility

The Environmental Impact Assessment application is supported on desktops and tablet devices with the following browsers: Microsoft Internet Explorer 8/9/10/11, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, and Apple Safari.
